${_('About')}

Throughout my undergraduate studies I have had subjects which I've found easy, and subjects which I have found difficult. This not a particularly groudbreaking statement to make, in fact, this sentiment is most probably the experience of all undergraduates. For me, I believe that only some of the experienced difficulty comes down to the underlying content of the subject being studied - the main difficulty, I found, has always been in the teaching style of the academic.

Naturally, I would seek to aid my understanding of subject material by searching the internet for resources which could support me. Frustratingly, many of the subjects that I was having trouble with had little to no alternative content on the web, which I thought was such a shame. This website has been created to address this need. Primarily, it seeks to provide simplistic explanations to complex material, with a focus on practical application of theory.
